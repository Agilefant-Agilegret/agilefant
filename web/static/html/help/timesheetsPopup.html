Enabling timesheets gives you the possibility to log the hours you spend on certain tasks, stories, iterations or projects.


Opening the spent effort dialog of a backlog.
Double clicking the ES cell opens the same dialog as the spent effort link in the edit menu.

By enabling the Timesheets feature, a new timesheets tab appears, which allows you to generate reports on the spent effort.

Note! Spent effort should not be confused with estimating stories or tasks. The spent effort is implemented for the sole purpose of tracking time usage, and is not in any way connected with altering the corresponding effort estimates.
